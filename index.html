About Us

We created Euphoric Melody Study Cafe to give students and those who are passionate about learning a place to study, while providing soothing background music.
Just interested in hanging out with friends or buying a something to drink? Everyone is welcome! We have a separate area for those wanting to just sit and chat. Be sure to check out our menu.

Why a Study Cafe?

So why a study cafe? The idea came from a trip to South Korea. Many students would study at cafes while enjoying something to drink.
We wanted to create something like that here, by giving students another option besides at home or at a library to study.
